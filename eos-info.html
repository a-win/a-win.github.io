Interoperable EOS Platform

This project is an attempt to build a low carbon footprint energy units trading platform on EOS because of the eassy tokenomics definiton on EOS and the focus on lightweight smart contracts.
The token value changes based on externalities affcting factors of production, but is primarily driven by the demand/supply determinant bidding system.
Since EOS is pushing through a long bearish phase and is taking time to mobilie strong market interest, the project is on hold. But our company is interested and is still working on
developing tools to make EOS interoperable with Hyperledger Fabric and our blockchain development tools for enterprise HLFabric.
